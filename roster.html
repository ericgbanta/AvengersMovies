In the Marvel Cinematic Universe, the Avengers were a team started by S.H.I.E.L.D director, Nick Fury. The team, labeled as "Earth's Mightiest Heroes", debuted on the big screen in Joss Whedon's 2012 film, The Avengers. This page contains a list of MCU Avenger characters. Details on the characters first appearance, number of appearances, origin etc. will also be listed.
